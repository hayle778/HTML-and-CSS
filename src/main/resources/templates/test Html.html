Crabcake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1lb.jumbo lump or backfin crab meat
  • 2 slices bread or bread crumbs
  • 1tbsp.mayonnaise(light or regular)
  • 2tsp.Old Bay seasoning
  • 1tbsp.snipped parsley
  • 1 egg(or substitute for special diets)
  • tartar sause or cocktail sauce

Directions:

Beat the egg in a bowl. Trim the crusts from the bread and break the slices into small pieces. Add these pieces to the egg. Mix in the mayonnaise, Dijion mustard, Chesapeake seasoning and prasley, and beat well. Place the crabmeat in the bowl and pour the egg mixture over the top. Gently toss and told the igredients together, taking care not to break up the lumbs of crabmeat.

Form the cake by hands into patties about 3 inches around and 3/4-inch thick. Shape should be like a cookie, not like a meat ball or a golf ball. Place the cakes in the refrigerator for at least 45 minutes before cooking. This is very important so the cakes don`t fall apart

Broil the Crab Cake:

Slip them under a preheated broiler until nicely browned, turning to cook evenly, about 4 to 5 minutes on each side

Or saute:

Heat a small amount of butter or olive oil in a skillet and saute the cakes, turning several times, until golden brown or about 8 minutes total cooking time.

Serve at once with tartar sauce, mustard, or cocktail sauce on the side.

Serves 6 people
